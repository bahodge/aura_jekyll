  • Patience

  • Empathy

  • Integrity

What we do

  • Coordinated care and support

    Set Appointments with Doctors, and other providers

    Coordinate transportation

    Coordinate trusted pet care

    Oversee other services(e.g., maintenance)

  • Manage budget and reduce the risk of fraud & exploitation

    Assess and organize monthly budget

    Oversee bill paying

    Assist in balancing bank accounts

    Provide fraud/scam prevention training and intervention

  • Health and Welfare

    Personal visits and care check-ups to ensure your loved one is being cared for as if you were there.

    Regular phone calls

  • Supervise Medical and Non-medical care

    Supervise & Manage service providers

    Attend Doctor’s appointments if warranted and report details to the family

  • Keep the family informed

    Provide detailed monthly updates to the family/Power of Attorney (POA) and brief summaries of each personal visit

    Provide incident reports as needed (hospital, change in health or mental status, or other significant matter)
